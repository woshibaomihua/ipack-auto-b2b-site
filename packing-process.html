Packing Process

Export packing process for fragile headlights, tail lights and auto body parts.

Why packing matters

Headlights, tail lights and lamp covers can be damaged during international transport if inner protection and carton strength are not controlled. This page helps buyers understand the packing requirements before bulk orders.

  • Use inner protection for fragile lens products
  • Confirm cartons and labels for mixed model orders
  • Separate left/right side parts clearly
  • Prepare packing list for B2B import buyers

How can I request a quote?

Use the contact form, inquiry list or WhatsApp button with vehicle, OE number and quantity details.

What information should I send?

Please send product category, vehicle make/model/year, OE number, side position, quantity, destination and product photos if available.
