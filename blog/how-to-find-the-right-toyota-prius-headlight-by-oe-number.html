How to Find the Right Toyota Prius Headlight by OE Number

Target keyword: Toyota Prius headlight OE number. This guide is written for importers, wholesalers, distributors and repair market suppliers.

ContentsDirect answerBuyer pain pointsQuotation checklistHow I-Pack helps

Direct answer

For Toyota Prius headlight OE number, the most important sourcing principle is to confirm fitment before price negotiation. Buyers should provide vehicle make, model, year, OE number, side position and product photo. This helps reduce mismatch, sample delays and return risk in bulk orders.

Buyer pain points

Auto lights and body parts are not generic items. A small model-year difference can change the lamp shape, mounting bracket, connector, lens style or side position. Importers and wholesalers often lose time when suppliers quote by product name only.

  • Vehicle model names may vary between markets.
  • OE numbers may have left and right side variations.
  • LHD/RHD lighting requirements may matter for some headlight products.
  • Fragile lamp products need better export packing than standard plastic parts.
