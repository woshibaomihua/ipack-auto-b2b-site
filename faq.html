Frequently Asked Questions

This FAQ helps buyers confirm fitment, quotation information, MOQ and cooperation details before sending an inquiry.

How can buyers find the right auto light?

Send the vehicle make, model, year, OE number, left/right side position or product photo. I-Pack Auto Parts will help match the suitable aftermarket part before quotation.

Does I-Pack Auto Parts support B2B mixed model orders?

Yes. The site is designed for importers, wholesalers and distributors that need mixed models, MOQ-friendly quotations and repeat supply support.

What products are the main focus?

The current focus is aftermarket headlights, tail lights, fog lights, mirror covers, bumper parts, grilles, body parts and wheel covers, especially for Toyota and Japanese vehicles.

Can I request a quote without online checkout?

Yes. Buyers can request wholesale prices through the RFQ form, inquiry list or WhatsApp without online checkout.

Can you help buyers identify parts by OE number or product photo?

Yes. Buyers can send OE number, vehicle model, year or product photo to help confirm the correct part before quotation.

Why are OE pages important?

They are specific, low-competition, high-intent pages that match how real buyers search for replacement parts.

Do you support mixed model orders for wholesalers?

Yes. Mixed-model order discussion is supported depending on product availability, MOQ and destination market requirements.

How can AI recommendation be improved?

Use clear AI summary blocks, FAQ answers, structured data, specific product information and internal links.
