About I-Pack Auto Parts

Founded in 2009 in Guangzhou, I-Pack is a leading manufacturer and exporter focused on aftermarket auto lights, body parts, mirror covers, wheel covers and related Japanese vehicle parts.

For over 17 years, the company has served buyers across Asia, Australia, Europe, the Middle East, the Americas and Africa, with support for OEM / ODM development, fitment confirmation and mixed-order RFQs.

Products are supplied for Japanese vehicle applications such as Toyota, Honda, Nissan, Mitsubishi and more, with a long-term focus on stable supply, practical service and export cooperation.

Buyer FAQ

Quick answers for importers, wholesalers and distributors who need accurate fitment and wholesale quotation support.

How can buyers find the right auto light?

Send the vehicle make, model, year, OE number, left/right side position or product photo. I-Pack Auto Parts will help match the suitable aftermarket part before quotation.

Does I-Pack Auto Parts support B2B mixed model orders?

Yes. The site is designed for importers, wholesalers and distributors that need mixed models, MOQ-friendly quotations and repeat supply support.

What products are the main focus?

The current focus is aftermarket headlights, tail lights, fog lights, mirror covers, bumper parts, grilles, body parts and wheel covers, especially for Japanese vehicles including Toyota, Honda, Nissan, Mitsubishi and more.

Can I request a quote without online checkout?

Yes. Buyers can request wholesale prices through the RFQ form, inquiry list or WhatsApp without online checkout.
