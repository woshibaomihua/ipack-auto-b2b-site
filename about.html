ABOUT US

Reliable Supply for Japanese Vehicle Parts

Founded in 2009 in Guangzhou, I-Pack is a manufacturer and exporter of aftermarket auto lights, body parts, mirror covers, wheel covers and related components for Japanese vehicle applications.

The company focuses on practical B2B service for importers, wholesalers, distributors, repair shops and aftermarket channels that need stable products, fast matching and export-oriented support.

With 17+ years of history, 20+ country agents, 30,500+ SKU items and 60+ countries served, I-Pack positions itself as a long-term cooperation partner rather than only a simple catalog supplier.
